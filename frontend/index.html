Frequently Asked Questions

Can this system run school and university elections?

Yes. Admins can configure election groups for schools, colleges, universities, organizations, and local councils.

How do I know my vote was counted?

Each submitted ballot receives a unique transaction reference in the system logs for verification and auditing.

Can one person vote more than once?

No. Duplicate ballot submission is blocked automatically per voter and per election.

Where can I get support in Tanzania?

Use the support channels on the contact page, including local phone and email assistance.
